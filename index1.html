Research & Guidance


Today, research has become a buzz word in academic fraternity. In every course of higher education there is a compulsory component of research.

Research is a process of collecting, analyzing and interpreting information to answer questions. It is a structured enquiry that utilizes acceptable scientific methodology to investigate in specific subject to solve problems and create new knowledge that is generally applicable. Research helps to establish facts or principles. We examine and explore various things in our daily life as well. However, the difference between our casual everyday exploration and educational research is that it holds the following characteristics: specific procedure, exactness, verifiability and validity. This controlled, rigorous, systematic process of collecting empirical and critical data can reveal unbiased and objective findings.

Social research refers to study conducted by social scientist in fields of social sciences and humanities. Different academic disciplines prefer different approaches, such as qualitative, quantitative, and experimental.

In Short


‘NOBLE Institute of Research Guidance’ helps the researcher to remain motivated during the process of research to ensure flawless and timely completion of the research project by providing necessary guidance to...

  • Select topic of interest to sustain the required motivation
  • Select a specific and clear topic that one can manage within the available time and resources
  • Bring about clarity about the indicators and measurement of concepts in the study
  • Develop expertise in the investigator for the proposed task
  • Direct preparation of research tool
  • Analyse the data correctly
  • Ensure that the study is relevant and useful in policy formulation and generalisations
  • Ensure correct report-writing using appropriate language, etc.
  • Guide the entire process of research investigation
